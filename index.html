About Us

On Demand is a mobile detailing service provider that caters to the auto detailing needs of our clients at their preferred location, be it their home or office. Our team of experienced and professional detailers is dedicated to delivering the best detailing services to our clients.

Mobile Service


We understand that life can be busy, and finding the time to take your car to a detailer can be challenging. That's why we bring our services to you. Our fully equipped mobile units are designed to provide a complete detailing experience, without having to leave your premises.

Professional Team


Our team is passionate about cars and providing the best possible experience for our clients. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and strive to make every car we work on look like new. Whether it's a quick wash or a full detailing service, we treat every vehicle with the same level of care and attention.

Affordable Luxury


At Island Auto Detailers, we are committed to sustainability. We don't rely on our clients' electricity or water, as we bring our own power and water supply. This approach allows us to deliver the same high-quality detailing services while minimizing our impact on the environment.
